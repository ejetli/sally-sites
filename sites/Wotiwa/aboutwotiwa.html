WOTIWA beauty is a beauty brand that carters for clients makeup irrespective of the event. WOTIWA means Worth The Wait because we are convinced clients are worth a distinctive treatment based on the jewels we know they are. Also, we want clients to be reassured that every single experience will be breathtaking-worth the wait. Behind the committed, heart-warming and professional work put in place is Hananeel (Hanni), a Freelance makeup artist based in Yaounde.

In 2015 Hanni realized that she was gifted in makeup artistry and therefore decided to take out time to work and improve on her skill. It was relatively easy for her to dedicate hours to craft out her talent and transcend it to profit making because makeup had been her long time passion. 3 years later-2018 She took the courageous step of calling her self a Makeup Artist. That's when the name WOTIWA Beauty, which is not a dialect or tribal name as popular opinion holds, for the first time, made sense to her. Whether it be Bridal makeup, makeup for day time or night events, WOTIWA Beauty prides herself in helping her clients to see the beauty in themselves through a look tailored to meet the desired needs of the individual. The journey,the experience, is WOTIWA
